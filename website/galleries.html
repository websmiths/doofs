This page contains photographic documentation organized into two categories:

  • Rubbish & Environmental Impact
  • Event Promotion Materials

Rubbish & Environmental Impact

This gallery documents the rubbish, litter, and environmental damage left behind after events. These images show the real impact on the local environment and demonstrate the inadequate cleanup and waste management practices.

Note: These images document the state of the area after events, showing litter, discarded items, and environmental damage that remains long after attendees have left.

Event Promotion Materials

This gallery documents promotional materials and advertisements for these events. These images demonstrate the scale of marketing, the commercial nature of operations, and the public promotion of events that operate without proper permits and oversight.

Note: These promotional materials show the professional marketing approach used to attract hundreds of attendees to events that lack the proper licensing, permits, and infrastructure required for commercial operations of this scale.
